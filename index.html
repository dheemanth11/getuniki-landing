Uniki

The cognitive seatbelt for ADHD.

Existing tools manage your intent (to-do lists, calendars). Uniki manages your context.

Using passive computer vision, Uniki detects when you drift from a task—like leaving the stove on or walking away from unfinished work—and gently nudges you back. No manual entry required.

Technical Approach

Ego-Centric Sensor Fusion

We use Viterbi state tracking to infer user location and intent without the jitter of raw GPS or basic object detection.

Privacy-First Architecture

Context is processed locally. Images are converted to vector embeddings (CLIP) on-device, preserving semantic meaning without storing video.
